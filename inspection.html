( CHINA ENTRY-EXIT INSPECTION AND QUARANTINE BUREAU )

In 1999, China Entry-exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau (“CIQ” for short) was founded by consolidating various local entry-exit health quarantine authorities, animal and plant quarantine authorities and import and export commodity inspection authorities. In 2002, General Administration of Quality Supervision, Inspection and Quarantine was officially established by consolidating China Entry-exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau and State Quality Supervision Bureau, which differs in its local branches. In general, CIQ (Inspection and Quarantine) are among national governing bodies while quality supervision bureaus are classified into local authorities. Additionally, the grass-roots branch bureaus vary in administrative levels. For instance, Jiangsu Taicang Entry-exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau is directly under the provincial bureau and among units at division level while Jiangsu Taicang Quality Supervision Bureau is among units at section level. Besides, the civil servants are recruited through different channels. Among them, the civil servants serving for Entry-exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau are recruited by taking national civil servant examination while the civil servants serving for Quality Supervision Bureau are recruited by taking local civil servant examination.

Job Responsibility of CIQ (Entry-exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau)

(1) To implement the rules, measures and working regulations of entry-exit health quarantine, animal and plant quarantine and import and export commodity inspection laws, statutes and policies and take on administrative law enforcement work such as entry-exit inspection and quarantine, certification and accreditation and regulations within jurisdiction.

(2) To take charge of conducting entry-exit health quarantine, infectious disease surveillance and health surveillance, undertaking prevention and control work of infectious diseases into port, giving  inward and outward personnel vaccination and taking on management of check up and surveillance of infectious diseases.

(3) To take charge of undertaking work such as quarantine and inspection of entry-exit animal, plants and products produced thereby or other quarantine objects, animal and plant epidemic monitoring and investigation and taking animal and plant epidemic emergency precaution and prevention.

(4) To take charge of statutory inspection and regulation of import and export commodities (including food), packaging inspection of general packaging and hazardous export goods, appraisal and management of import and export commodities, assets valuation for foreign investors and re-inspection of import and export commodities.

(5) To take charge of obtaining health quarantine registration of units for producing (breeding and planting), processing and storing import and export food, animals, plants and products produced thereby, issuing import and export quality assurance license and export quality assurance license and undertaking and regulating certification and accreditation of import and export products and laboratory, personnel registration and other work.

(6) To take charge of entry verification of civilian goods under the import licensing system and outbound verification of export commodities or commodities in transit.

(7) To take charge of health surveillance, quarantine and appropriate load testing and evaluation of entry-exit transport vehicles, containers and vessels and management of disinfection used in entry-exit transport vehicles, containers, packaging, bedding materials and goods.

(8) To take charge of implementing relative international agreements, contracts and protocols signed by state or state council departments and China Entry-exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau and the agreements on technical barrier to trade and quarantine agreements.

(9) To take charge of issuance of marks and seals of entry-exit inspection and quarantine certificate and granting GSP Origin Certificates for Export Products and Certificates of Origin.

(10) To take charge of entry-exit inspection and quarantine statistics, investigate and garner data on overseas infectious diseases, animal and plant epidemic and commodity quality in international trade and provide relative information.

(11) To take charge of regulating quarantine, accreditation and certification bodies (including Sino-foreign joint or cooperative institutions) and disinfection agencies.

(12) To undertake other tasks assigned by superior entry-exit inspection and quarantine bureaus.
